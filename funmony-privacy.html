Privacy Policy

Privacy policy for Funmony.

This page is the public privacy policy URL for Funmony by Keetane Lab. If the app's data handling changes in a future release, this page will be updated before or with that release.

Last updated: April 19, 2026

Scope

This privacy policy applies to the current release of Funmony on iPhone and iPad.

Information We Store

Funmony stores learning data locally on the device.

  • Profiles, settings, reminders, level progress, session history, and answer logs are stored locally.
  • Daily summary exports and backup files can be written to Files when the person using the app chooses to export them.
  • Secure local storage may be used for purchase flags and related app settings.

Server Communication

The current release of Funmony does not require account registration.

The app does not send learning records, profile data, or answer logs to Keetane Lab servers from inside the app in the current release.

Notifications

Funmony can schedule local reminder notifications if the person using the app grants notification permission.

Reminder times are stored locally and are used only to schedule local notifications on the device.

Files, Exports, and Backups

CSV, PDF, and backup exports are saved to a user-visible Files location selected by the app's local export flow.

Those files are controlled by the person using the device. Keetane Lab does not operate its own cloud sync service for Funmony in the current release.

Purchases and Advertising

Funmony may offer optional paid features such as unlocking additional functionality.

If advertising or purchase implementation details change in a future release, this page will be updated accordingly.

Crash Information

Keetane Lab may review crash reports that are made available through Apple's standard developer tools, such as App Store Connect, TestFlight, or Xcode Organizer.

The current release does not rely on a separate third-party analytics SDK for user behavior tracking.

Data Deletion

Because app data is stored locally, deleting the app removes local app data from the device.

If a person exported CSV, PDF, or backup files to Files, they can also delete those files from the chosen location.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to nobodyknowsxx@gmail.com.

Product support for Funmony is available on the support page.
