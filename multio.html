Multio

Practice long multiplication one cell at a time.

Multio helps children work through long multiplication with a guided board, step-by-step input, immediate review, and local progress tracking for each profile.

See screenshots Support

Learning flow

A focused practice loop for written multiplication.

Children choose a level, solve each cell in order, then review mistakes with the completed layout visible.

Guided long multiplication

The board highlights the current cell and keeps the calculation sequence visible from partial products to the final answer.

Profiles and levels

Each child has a local profile. The displayed level follows the highest stage completed with a perfect set.

Review and reminders

Missed problems are saved for review, and optional local reminders help families keep a short daily routine.
