Privacy Policy

Privacy policy for DoReMi Palette.

This page is the public privacy policy URL for DoReMi Palette by Keetane Lab.

Last updated: May 14, 2026

Scope

This privacy policy applies to the planned iPhone and iPad release of DoReMi Palette.

Information We Store

DoReMi Palette is designed as a local-first sheet music coloring app.

  • Imported sheet music files, rendering settings, color settings, and related app preferences may be stored locally on the device.
  • Files selected by the person using the app remain under that person's control.
  • Secure local storage may be used for purchase flags or app settings if paid features are added.

Server Communication

The planned initial release does not require account registration.

DoReMi Palette is not planned to send imported sheet music, coloring settings, or document contents to Keetane Lab servers from inside the app.

Files and Documents

Imported MusicXML or related sheet music files are handled locally for display and coloring.

If export or sharing features are added, exported files will be controlled by the person using the device.

Purchases and Advertising

DoReMi Palette may offer optional paid features in a future release.

If advertising or purchase implementation details change, this page will be updated accordingly.

Crash Information

Keetane Lab may review crash reports made available through Apple's standard developer tools, such as App Store Connect, TestFlight, or Xcode Organizer.

The planned initial release does not rely on a separate third-party analytics SDK for user behavior tracking.

Data Deletion

Because app data is planned to be stored locally, deleting the app removes local app data from the device.

Files exported or stored outside the app can be deleted from their chosen locations by the person using the device.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to nobodyknowsxx@gmail.com.

Product support for DoReMi Palette is available on the support page.
