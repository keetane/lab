Preparing for App Store

Learning flow

A focused practice loop for written subtraction.

Children choose a subtraction format, follow each borrowing and answer step in order, then review the completed written work.

Guided written subtraction

The board highlights the current answer, borrow helper, or reduced digit so learners can follow the subtraction algorithm.

Borrowing step by step

Borrowing is separated into the helper one, slash mark, reduced upper digit, and final answer input.

Review after each set

Every problem remains visible in review, with incorrect items clearly marked.
