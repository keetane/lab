Chapter 01

Start with silence, then remove more.

The Apple-like frame works best when copy is compressed and the surrounding space does the pacing. Keep the main thought singular, and let the next section carry the next idea.

Let light surfaces act as punctuation.

Even in a dark-mode-first site, selective light cards create rhythm. They should mark emphasis, not become a second theme.

Chapter 03

Interaction stays blue, and only blue.

The site uses one accent to make intent obvious. Buttons, focus rings, and inline actions are legible because nothing else competes for attention.

The current product names and story copy are placeholders. What matters is that the structure is strong enough to accept the real material later without redesigning the shell.
