Studio Keetane Privacy Policy

Studio Keetane is a static prompt gallery. It helps you prepare text prompts for image-generation services such as ChatGPT, Gemini, and Grok.

Information we handle

Studio Keetane does not require an account and does not store uploaded photos, generated prompts, or prompt edits on our servers. Prompt text is edited in your browser and can be copied to your clipboard.

External services

Links to ChatGPT, Gemini, Grok, and source pages open external websites. Any text, photos, or files you provide to those services are handled under their own terms and privacy policies.

Clipboard

When you use copy or open-in-service buttons, Studio Keetane may write the current prompt to your clipboard so you can paste it into the selected service.

Analytics and contact

This page does not intentionally collect personal information. If you contact us by email, we use your message and email address only to respond to your inquiry.
