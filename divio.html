Learning flow

A guided loop for written division.

Children solve each step in order, from quotient selection to multiplication, subtraction, bringing down, and checking the result.

Guided long division

The board highlights the current step and keeps the division algorithm visible so learners can follow the sequence without guessing the next action.

Profiles and levels

Each child has a local profile. The displayed level follows the highest stage completed with a perfect set.

Review and reminders

Missed problems are saved for review, and optional local reminders help families keep a short daily routine.
