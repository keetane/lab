Chromatone

Train your ear through color and chords.

Chromatone is a chord-first ear-training app for children and adults. It links fixed colors to fixed chord sounds, turns practice into a quick game loop, and helps learners build recognition through repetition.

See features Back to home

Why Chromatone

A game loop designed for daily listening practice.

The app keeps the core cycle short: hear one chord, choose one color, get immediate feedback, then continue.

Chord-first training

Chromatone starts from fixed color and chord pairs so learners can build stable recognition patterns from the beginning.

Level-based unlocks

Practice can grow one sound at a time, while advanced learners can inspect a fully unlocked set for verification and review.

Daily review

Weakest sounds, weekly summaries, daily reports, reminders, and Files export support make the app usable beyond a single session.

For families and learners

Simple enough for a child, structured enough for deliberate practice.

Chromatone is intended for short, repeatable sessions. Profiles, reminders, daily summaries, and practice history help parents and learners keep progress visible without making the product heavy.

Release scope

Built for iPhone and iPad with local-first data handling.

The current product stores profiles, settings, session results, and exports locally. Reminder scheduling and Files-based export are included so the app can work as a self-contained study tool.
