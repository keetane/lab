Studio Keetane Support

Studio Keetane provides prompt examples. Image generation, uploaded files, and final results are handled by the external AI service you choose.

How to use

Select a card, adjust the pattern or color notes, copy the prompt, then paste it into ChatGPT, Gemini, or Grok together with your reference image.

When results differ

Generated images may vary by service and model. If the output is too different, add concrete notes about layout, number of panels, language, photo angle, or what must not change.

Photo guidance

Use a clear, front-facing image when possible. For style comparisons, avoid heavy shadows, extreme angles, or photos where hair, glasses, or clothing are hidden.

Contact

For broken links, wording issues, or prompt improvements, contact us by email and include the card name and the page language.
