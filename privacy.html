Privacy Policy

Common privacy policy for current and upcoming Keetane Lab apps.

This page is intended to serve as the public privacy policy URL for Keetane Lab apps, including Kids Money, Multio, Divio, and future releases. Update this page if the actual data handling changes.

Scope

This privacy policy applies to Keetane Lab apps, including Kids Money and future apps such as Multio and Divio.

If a specific app later requires different privacy terms, those app-specific details should be added here or linked clearly from this page.

Information We Collect

For the initial release period, Keetane Lab intends to operate the current apps without collecting user data from the app for analytics, advertising, or external crash reporting.

  • For Kids Money, app content such as child profiles, chores, allowance history, and related settings is intended to be stored locally on the device.
  • Some local app state, such as premium-related state if used in the future, may also be stored locally on the device.
  • No external analytics SDK is intended to be used in the initial release.
  • No external advertising SDK is intended to be used in the initial release.

Based on the current release plan, Keetane Lab expects the initial App Store privacy submission for Kids Money to be based on no app-collected data.

Files and Exported Data

Keetane Lab apps may provide export or save flows through the Files interface.

When a person exports data using Files, they may choose a local device location or a cloud-backed location such as iCloud Drive or another supported file provider.

That storage destination is chosen by the person using the app. Keetane Lab does not treat this as the same thing as operating its own cloud sync service.

Crash Information

Keetane Lab may review crash information made available through Apple's standard developer tools, such as crash reports visible through App Store Connect, TestFlight, or Xcode Organizer.

No separate third-party crash reporting SDK is intended for the initial release.

Purchases and Advertising

Paid features are not intended to be enabled in the initial release period. Keetane Lab may review monetization options later and update this policy before enabling them.

Advertising is not intended to be enabled in the initial release period.

Children's Data

Some Keetane Lab apps may be designed for children or for family use. When that is the case, data practices must be reviewed with child privacy requirements in mind before release.

If an app is directed to children, Keetane Lab should verify that the final release behavior and App Store privacy answers match child privacy requirements in the relevant territories.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to nobodyknowsxx@gmail.com.
