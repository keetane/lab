Color sheet music so notes become easier to see.

DoReMi Palette is a sheet music coloring app built with DoReMiRenderer. It helps learners read notes by applying clear, consistent colors to music notation.

New release Back to home

Why DoReMi Palette

A calmer way to approach notation.

The app is designed for learners who benefit from visual anchors while reading music.

Color-coded notes

Apply a consistent color palette to notes so pitch recognition can become more immediate.

MusicXML based

The app is built around imported sheet music and the rendering foundation of DoReMiRenderer.

Practice focused

Readable notation, predictable colors, and a quiet interface keep attention on the music.

For students and families

Make sheet music feel less crowded.

DoReMi Palette is being prepared as a tool for reading practice, home study, and early music learning where color can reduce friction without changing the music itself.
